Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh are your fruits?

Our fruits are harvested at peak ripeness and delivered within 24-48 hours. We work directly with farmers to ensure maximum freshness.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all 50 states. Delivery times may vary based on your location.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 100% freshness guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund or replace your order.

Do you offer organic fruits?

Yes! All our fruits are certified organic and grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also check your order status in your account.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it. Contact us immediately for assistance.
